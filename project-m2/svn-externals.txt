Tip: how to include UJO Framework to your SVN project structure:
****************************************************************
Set the subversion property 'svn:externals' to value: 
   ujo-framework https://ujoframework.svn.sourceforge.net/svnroot/ujoframework/trunk/project-m2

on your local Project directory and call statement: 
   svn update
   
More information: 
   http://svnbook.red-bean.com/en/1.0/ch07s03.html
